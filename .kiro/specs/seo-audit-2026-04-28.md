## What's working

- Homepage clear value prop: $999 flat-fee MLS, $1,399 total vs 5% commission
- Real structure: Sell/Buy/Rent/Invest/Areas/News/Blog/tools
- Mesa area page with market stats, ZIP breakdowns, CTAs
- Tools exist (Home Value, Net Sheet, AI Listing, Affordability, Comparison)

## What's failing

### Critical (addressed this session)

- **`/blog/selling-guides/` empty** — the highest-leverage SEO gap. Action:
  6 canonical seller guides drafted this session. Tracked in
  `.kiro/specs/seller-hub-content.md`.

### Still open

- **Market snapshot dashes** — when Zillow data is missing, we show `—`.
  Trust signal concern for crawlers. Action: show "updated quarterly" or
  a range instead of dashes.
- **Agent bios + license numbers** — about page exists but thin.
  Action: flesh out `/about/` with owner photo, ADRE license, service
  area map.
- **Real testimonials** — reviews page placeholder. Need 10+ real client
  quotes with names once Google Business Profile is live.
- **Google Business Profile** — still incomplete (owner action).
- **ZIP-level pages** — `/areas/mesa/` good, but no per-ZIP pages
  (85201, 85203, 85204, etc). Action: Phase 2.

## Target queries we should win

Not homes-for-sale. Zillow owns that. We target:
- "flat fee MLS Mesa AZ"
- "how to sell a house in Mesa AZ"
- "Mesa AZ seller closing costs"
- "sell home without realtor Mesa"
- "Mesa seller net sheet"
- "FSBO vs flat fee MLS Mesa"
- "best time to sell a house in Mesa"
- "Mesa home value 85203" (and other ZIPs)
